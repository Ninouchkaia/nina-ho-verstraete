Vision & Overview

I am a bioinformatics researcher and data scientist specializing in functional genomics, computational modeling, and biological data integration. My work bridges academic research and industrial applications, with a focus on life sciences, complex systems, and technological innovation.

Research Themes

Cancer Research & Immunotherapy

  • Agent-based modeling of tumor-associated macrophages (TAMs) in chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL).
  • Prediction of immunotherapy response through tumor microenvironment deconvolution (GEM-DeCan).
  • Multi-omics studies on metabolic resistance in acute myeloid leukemia (AML).

Network Medicine & COVID-19

  • CovMulNet19 project: integrating proteins, diseases, drugs, and symptoms for COVID-19 analysis.
  • Multiscale modeling of SARS-CoV-2 interactome using statistical physics.

Systems Biology & Modeling

  • NetLogo and OpenMole for agent-based simulations.
  • Integration of network modeling with clinical and molecular data.

Transcriptional Regulation & Structural Bioinformatics

  • Study of P-TEFb regulation via interactions with HEXIM1 and HIV-1 Tat.
  • Analysis of ankyrin repeat proteins, motif detection, and structural-functional modeling.

Data Integration & Infrastructure

  • Pipeline development for sequence analysis and enrichment statistics.
  • Biological metadata curation and integration with SQL and web scraping tools.

Tools & Technologies

  • Modeling: NetLogo, OpenMole
  • Bioinfo: MySQL, statistical analysis, multiple sequence alignment
  • Dev: Java JEE, Shell, Tomcat, Eclipse, SOAP, Oracle SQL
  • Experimental: FLIP, FRAP, FRET, yeast two-hybrid, confocal microscopy
  • Management: Agile Scrum, documentation & automated testing
