Overview

I am a bioinformatics researcher and data scientist specialized in functional genomics, computational modeling, and data integration across biological systems. My work spans both academic research and industrial applications, at the intersection of life sciences, complex systems modeling, and information technologies.

Bioinformatics & Computational Research

Postdoctoral Researcher | INSERM - Cancer Research Center of Toulouse (2019–2023)

  • Agent-based modeling of cellular interactions within tumor microenvironments (NetLogo, OpenMole).
  • Study of monocyte differentiation into macrophages in chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL).
  • LungPredict Project: Tumor microenvironment deconvolution and immunotherapy response prediction.
  • Multi-omics integration for metabolic resistance in acute myeloid leukemia.
  • COVID-19 network medicine and therapeutic discovery.

Postdoctoral Researcher | CONICET - University of Buenos Aires (2013–2015)

  • Structural and sequence analysis of repeat-motif proteins involved in transcriptional regulation.
  • Development of pipelines for motif detection, multiple sequence alignment, and statistical enrichment analysis.
  • Metadata management and biological database integration (MySQL, web scraping).

PhD Research | CNRS - Ecole Normale Supérieure - Paris (2009–2012)

  • Functional characterization of the Positive Transcription Elongation Factor (P-TEFb) and its interaction with HEXIM1 and HIV-1 Tat proteins.
  • Techniques: molecular biology, confocal microscopy (FLIP, FRAP, FRET), yeast two-hybrid screening.

Software Engineering Experience

Software Engineer | Capgemini - Airbus Defense & Space (2017–2019)
