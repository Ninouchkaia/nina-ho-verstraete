Publications

RNA Biology and Viral Transcription

Studies on 7SK RNA and its role in transcriptional regulation, in metazoans and in the context of HIV infection.

  • Marz, M., Donath, A., Verstraete, N., Nguyen, V. T., Stadler, P. F., & Bensaude, O. (2009). Evolution of 7SK RNA and its protein partners in metazoa. Molecular Biology and Evolution, 26(12), 2821–2830.
  • Verstraete, N., Kuzmina, A., Diribarne, G., Nguyen, V. T., Kobbi, L., Ludanyi, M., Taube, R., & Bensaude, O. (2014). A Cyclin T1 point mutation that abolishes positive transcription elongation factor (P-TEFb) binding to Hexim1 and HIV Tat. Retrovirology, 11(1), 50.

Protein Evolution and Bioinformatics

Computational analyses of protein evolution and structure, including the metabolic trade-off in proteomes and the characterization of repeated protein motifs.

  • Krick, T., Verstraete, N., Alonso, L. G., Shub, D. A., Ferreiro, D. U., Shub, M., & Sánchez, I. E. (2014). Amino acid metabolism conflicts with protein diversity in natural proteomes. Molecular Biology and Evolution, 31(11), 2905–2912.
  • Parra, R. G., Espada, R., Verstraete, N., & Ferreiro, D. U. (2015). Structural and energetic characterization of the ankyrin repeat protein family. PLOS Computational Biology, 11(12), e1004659.

Biological Networks and COVID-19

Network biology approaches to study COVID-19, integrating multi-scale data (molecular interactions, diseases, symptoms) and comparing SARS-CoV-2 to other viruses.

  • Verstraete, N., Jurman, G., Bertagnolli, G., Ghavasieh, A., Pancaldi, V., & De Domenico, M. (2020). CovMulNet19, integrating proteins, diseases, drugs, and symptoms: a network medicine approach to COVID-19. Network and Systems Medicine, 3(1), 130–141.
  • Ghavasieh, A., Bontorin, S., Artime, O., Verstraete, N., & De Domenico, M. (2021). Multiscale statistical physics of the pan-viral interactome unravels the systemic nature of SARS-CoV-2 infections. Communications Physics, 4(1), 83.

Tumor Immunology and Cell Modeling

Mathematical modeling and multi-omics approaches to study tumor immune microenvironments and macrophage polarization, supporting predictions of immunotherapy responses.

  • Marku, M., Verstraete, N., Raynal, F., Madrid-Mencía, M., Domagala, M., Fournié, J.-J., Ysebaert, L., Poupot, M., & Pancaldi, V. (2020). Insights on TAM formation from a Boolean model of macrophage polarization based on in vitro studies. Cancers, 12(12), 3664.
  • Xie, T., Solórzano, J., Madrid-Mencía, M., Essabbar, A., Pernet, J., Kuo, M., Hucteau, A>, Coullomb, A>, Delfour, O>, Cruzalegui, F>, Verstraete, N>, & Pancaldi, V. (2021). GEM-DeCan: Improved tumor immune microenvironment profiling through novel gene expression and DNA methylation signatures predicts immunotherapy response (preprint). bioRxiv.
  • Verstraete, N>, Marku, M>, Domagala, M>, Arduin, H>, Bordenave, J>, Fournié, J.-J>, Ysebaert, L>, Poupot, M>, & Pancaldi, V> (2023) | An agent-based model of monocyte differentiation into tumour-associated macrophages in chronic lymphocytic leukemia. iScience, 26(6), 106897.

Interdisciplinary Projects

Design of a gesture-based interface for audiovisual performance, blending technological innovation and artistic creation.

  • Fernandez, J. M., Köppel, T., Verstraete, N>, Lorieux, G>, Vert, A>, & Spiesser, P> (2017). GeKiPe: a gesture-based interface for audiovisual performance. In Proceedings of the International Conference on New Interfaces for Musical Expression (NIME 2017), 450–455.
